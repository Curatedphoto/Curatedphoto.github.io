Info & Rates

Contact

We specialize in portrait and family photography, ranging from high school/college seniors to engagements to newborn photography. Sessions range from 30 - 60 minutes and is at a location of your choosing.

 

A fee of 50% of the total session price will be required upon scheduling. The remaining balance will be due at the end of the session.

 

Photos will be delivered as edited digital copies in a Google Photo album within 4 weeks of the session date. The final images will be yours to print, post and cherish as you choose. But please do not edit the photos as that is part of our art and craft. If you do post the photos on social media, we invite you to tag us (@curatedphoto) this helps people find us and get pictures that they too can cherish.
